
# 🧠 AI Digital Twin

> **Write once. Your Twin writes forever.**

AI Digital Twin is an intelligent web application that creates a personalized AI assistant capable of writing in your unique style. Instead of generating generic AI responses, it adapts to your communication patterns to draft emails, project plans, reports, assignments, and professional documents that feel authentically written by you.

Designed for both students and professionals, AI Digital Twin combines personalized writing assistance with AI detection and humanization tools in a futuristic, immersive interface.

## 💡 Inspiration

Writing assistants today generate content that often feels generic and lacks individuality. AI Digital Twin was created to solve that problem by helping users communicate in their own voice while saving time on repetitive writing tasks.

Our vision is simple:

> **Don't replace your voice. Scale it.**
